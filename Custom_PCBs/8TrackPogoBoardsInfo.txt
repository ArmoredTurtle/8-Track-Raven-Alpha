The 5V port on these boards is intended for future shenanigans and does not need to be hooked to anything.
It is common to the 5V and GND going to the servo.

Identification: There is A-Side and B-Side, you can identify this by the side that the JST connectors are on, not the side that the pogo is on.
B-side boards have the male pogos and go on your hub (raven or other) A-side boards have the female pogos and are for the cassettes.
